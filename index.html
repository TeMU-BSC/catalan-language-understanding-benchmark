What is CLUB?

The CLUB benchmark consists of 6 tasks: Named Entity Recognition (NER), Part-of-Speech Tagging (POS), Semantic Textual Similarity (STS), Text Classification (TC), Textual Entailment (TE), and Question Answering (QA).
